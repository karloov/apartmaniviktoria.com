Apartman Karlo

About the apartment

Apartments Viktoria consists of two completely separated apartments. Apartment Karlo has a kitchen, living room, and one bedroom with a bathroom. The total area is 40 square meters. The apartment is equipped with all new furniture, new pots and cutlery, a TV, an Iron, a Hair dryer, and AC. Behind the house, there is a terrace with a beautiful view that looks over the whole Kvarner and a big outdoor table. The distance to Lovran is 3 km (5 minutes by car). It is important to say that the parking in the whole of Lovran is available and free. All pets are welcome (the pet fee is 7 euros per day). Also, if you have any questions about the apartment or if you want to know the best places to go, you can always ask the host who lives on the ground floor. If you are looking for a peaceful non-crowded place in the middle of nature, but you also want to be close to the city center. Apartments Viktoria is the ideal place for your holiday.
